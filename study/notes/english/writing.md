## 一、小作文（Part A）
### 2021 建议信
Dear Friend,
I am writing to offer you some suggestions on job hunting in China.

First, you’d better polish your resume and highlight your strengths, especially your English proficiency and cross-cultural skills. Second, dress formally for interviews, as Chinese employers attach great importance to first impressions. Third, learn some basic Chinese phrases to facilitate communication.

I hope my advice will be helpful. If you have any questions, feel free to contact me.

Yours sincerely,
Li Ming

## 二、大作文（Part B）
### 2021 家庭教育/陪伴
As is vividly shown in the cartoon, a little boy asks his father to play with him, but the father, glued to his mobile phone, refuses coldly. This scene is thought-provoking.

The cartoon reveals a common phenomenon: many parents are so obsessed with electronic devices that they neglect their children. This trend is harmful. For one thing, it weakens parent-child bond and makes children feel lonely. For another, it may lead to children’s lack of emotional communication.

To address this problem, parents should reduce screen time and spend more quality time with their kids. Only in this way can children grow up healthily and happily.

---

### 2022 坚持/毅力
The cartoon depicts two young men climbing a mountain. One gives up halfway, while the other persists and finally reaches the summit. The caption reads, “Perseverance is victory.”

The message conveyed is clear: perseverance is the key to success. In our life, we inevitably encounter difficulties and setbacks. It is those who persist that are more likely to achieve their goals.

As far as I’m concerned, we should cultivate the spirit of perseverance. When facing challenges, we must stay determined and never give up. Only by persevering can we overcome obstacles.

---

### 2023 科技与人际关系
In the picture, a family gathers around a dining table, but instead of talking to each other, everyone is absorbed in their mobile phones. The atmosphere is cold and silent.

This picture reflects a social issue: modern technology is alienating interpersonal relationships. While smartphones bring convenience, they also make people addicted to the virtual world and lack face-to-face communication.

To reverse this trend, we should use technology rationally and prioritize real-life interactions. We should put down our phones during gatherings and communicate more with people around us.

---

### 2024 网络谣言/信息素养
The cartoon shows a person spreading false information online, while another person looks shocked and confused. It reveals the serious problem of online rumors in the digital age.

Online rumors are harmful. They mislead the public, damage reputation and even cause social panic. Some people spread rumors out of malice, while others lack the ability to distinguish right from wrong.

To tackle this problem, joint efforts are needed. The government should strengthen supervision, platforms should filter false information, and individuals should improve media literacy.

---

### 2025 耐用消费品拥有量
The chart clearly illustrates the changes in the ownership of major durable goods per 100 households in China from 2014 to 2023. All kinds of appliances increased steadily.

This trend reflects China’s rapid economic development and improving living standards. With rising income, ordinary families can afford more household appliances. Meanwhile, technological progress makes products more accessible.

In my view, this positive trend will continue. People’s quality of life will be further improved, and we should also pay attention to energy conservation and environmental protection.
